Double You: Clone Videos in Minutes

Record twice, paint to reveal, export your double.

Double You lets you create the classic clone effect so you appear alongside yourself in a single video. No AI, no gimmicks, just a focused tool that does one thing very well.

How it works

  1. Place your phone on a tripod or stable surface.
  2. Record your first take.
  3. Record your second take (do not move the phone).
  4. Paint on screen to reveal where each version of you appears.
  5. Export and share.
